About The Scrap Company

We are a leading recycling company that specializes in brokering copper, aluminum, and other recyclable materials. Our mission is to promote sustainability by providing environmentally friendly solutions for businesses and individuals alike.

With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality service tailored to each client. Whether you need to recycle a small amount of scrap metal or want a long-term partner to help manage the full recycling process, our team is here to help.

Our Mission

Promote sustainability through practical, responsible, and accessible recycling solutions.

Who We Serve

Businesses and individuals across Houston who need dependable recycling and brokerage support.

How We Work

1. Assess

We review your material type, quantity, and timeline.

2. Recommend

We provide practical recycling/brokerage options for your goals.

3. Execute

We coordinate the process and keep communication clear throughout.
